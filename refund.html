Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. Service Satisfaction

At AIJJJSQ7 Solutions LLC, we are committed to delivering high-quality consulting services. We understand that the success of our business depends on your satisfaction with our services.

2. Refund Eligibility

Our refund policy for consulting services is as follows:

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of service delivery
  • Services must not have been fully utilized or implemented
  • Client must provide detailed reasoning for dissatisfaction
  • Each case will be reviewed individually based on merit

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Custom research reports that have been delivered
  • Consulting hours that have been utilized
  • Implementation services that have been completed
  • Training sessions that have been conducted

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Provide your service details and reason for refund
  3. Submit any relevant documentation
  4. Allow 5-7 business days for review

If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.

5. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with our refund decision, we offer a dispute resolution process:

  • Submit a formal appeal within 14 days
  • Provide additional documentation if required
  • Schedule a discussion with our management team
  • Final decision will be made within 10 business days
